Welcome to Sunshine Academy

Sunshine Academy was established in 2016 by Mr Suleiman Isamotu Ajao who is the proprietor. Mr Suleiman is also an ex teacher with broad humanity and deep love and tolerance for his fellow men and children. The mission of the school was to provide students in Iwo with a quality, independent education thatb focused on engaging and active learning commitment to the enviroment, and dedication to the school and local communities. Located just north and south of iwo, The people of Telemu opened their doors with children of the community twenty stundents enrolled in creche while thirty enrolled in higher classes. Since its opening, the school's dynamic educational eviroment has continued to evolve.
