Message From The Proprietor


Dear Prospective Parents,


We are delighted that you are considering Sunshine Academy for your child’s future. This school has honed and finetuned a learning and life-skilling system that has been envied and benchmarked across Iwo and the region for many years.

An investment made in Sunshine Academy education pays many dividends through life. From the moment he enters the school gates, a child gets to realise who he is; and where his potential lies. Irrespective of his background or calibre, he is groomed and nurtured in a way that he can stride into the adult world, confidently, responsibly and independently. Multiple generations of successful students will be living proof that this unique learning system works, and works well and we also want your child to be a living proof.

Yet, in today’s world, no school of repute and quality can afford to sit on its laurels. So, a decisive shift is being engineered that will help Iwo's best school and the soon to be Nigeria's best school gain a global reputation, and go where no school from Iwo has ever gone.

In the next five years, therefore, the focus will be on academic excellence. This will help students get into the finest higher education institutions in Nigeria and around the world. Initiatives are also being put in place that will allow students to fit into a rapidly changing adult world. At the same time, the school’s big strength — pedagogy balanced with pastoral care — will be supported in equal measure to enhance the school’s standing as the Best School in Nigeria.

Unfortunately, when it comes to quality and excellence, there are no free lunches, nor can there be any compromises. To ensure that Sunshine Academy scales newer peaks, fees will necessarily need to be raised from time to time. As our potential parent partners, we look forward to your support on this. Of course, for an institution with a mission to enable all learner's access to learning we also have the means to offer financial assistance in the form of needs-based scholarships.

Should you require any financial clarification or assistance, please do not hesitate to contact the school in advance of your child’s arrival to further understand the financial assistance on offer and how the school can assist you in this matter. With all good wishes,.
